My Gym Program is called The Journey, formulated by Greg Nuckols it is as follows

Day 1 Day 2 Day 3 Day 4
Squat 6x3, 5-10lbs heavier than you've gone before OHP 6x3, 5-10lbs, heavier than you've gone before Deadlift 6x3, 5-10lbs heavier than you've gone before Bench Press 6x3, 5-10lbs heavier than you've gone before
Close Grip Bench Press 5x4,starting with 60% of your bench press load Front Squat 5x4, starting with 60% of your back squat load, and trying to add weight each week Push Press 5x4, starting with 90% of your OHP load Opposite stance deadlift 5x4, starting with 60% of your normal deadlift load
Romanian Deadlift 4x6, starting with 50% of your deadlift load Dips Bodyweight, 3 sets of as many clean reps as possible. Add external load when you're getting at least 12 reps on your third set Paused Squat 4x2m starting with 60% of your back squat load, trying to add weight each week Dumbell OHP 4x6, starting with 30% of your OHP load in each hand
